I'm a PhD student in the department of linguistics at the University of Connecticut . Before that I received my BA in philosophy at the University of Toronto .
My research mainly focuses on syntax, morphology, and the interaction between the two. My work involves both close investion of specific languages, especially Turkic languages, as well as cross-linguistic comparative research. Some topics I've been interested in recently include: cross-clausal argument licensing, and the interaction between head-movement and ellipsis.
