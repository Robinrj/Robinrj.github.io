I'm a PhD student in the department of linguistics at the University of Connecticut Before that I received my BA in philosophy at the University of Toronto .
My research mainly focuses on syntax, morphology, and the interaction between the two. In particular, I am interested in cross-linguistic variation regarding particular phenomena, e.g. cross-clausal licensing/ECM, verb-straning ellipsis, differential object marking, and what these points of variation reflect regarding underlying differences in grammatical architecture.
